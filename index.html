Our Services

Energy Guard specializes in about how to conserve energy and save money.It will help you to increase the comfort of your home, make a safer and healthier environment to live in and make your home more durable to last well in to the future. Energy audits protect the environment by reducing waste and pollution.

  •  Insulate

    When you properly insulate your home, you will keep heat inside during the winter and keep it outside during the summer. With our skills, we will properly install these insulation products for maximum efficiency. We will find air boundaries within your home and insulate where needed.

  •   Sealant

    With our blower door test and infra-red thermal imaging we locate openings to prevent leaks. This will lower your heating and cooling costs and keep your home at a more stable temperature. After our products are installed we use our blower door test to ensure maximum efficiency.

  •   Ice Dams

    In many houses, indoor air will pressurize toward the exterior of home or building and escape through openings, often toward the roof and into attics. We will properly insulate your attic to prevent ice dams and moisture from forming in upper levels of your home.
